Management Skills

My management style is rooted in strategic planning, adaptability, and effective communication, enabling me to deliver results even in challenging environments.

Project Management

At CHAMPS Bangladesh, I have managed multiple research projects, from study design to execution. These projects required meticulous planning, task prioritization, and the ability to adapt to unforeseen challenges, ensuring objectives were met on time and within resource constraints.
While deploying a software-based inpatient management system at Faridpur Medical College Hospital, I managed all aspects of the project, including stakeholder engagement, system development, user training, and troubleshooting. This required balancing technical demands with user needs and delivering the project ahead of schedule.

Team Coordination and Leadership

I have led multidisciplinary teams, including physicians, consent takers, medical technologists, and technical staff, fostering collaboration and ensuring that all team members were aligned with project goals. My ability to delegate effectively, provide guidance, and motivate my team has been instrumental in achieving project success. As a MITS team supervisor, I coordinate the efforts of physicians conducting minimally invasive tissue sampling, ensuring compliance with protocols and high-quality data collection.

Training and Capacity Building

I have successfully organized and facilitated training programs for healthcare professionals, including Research Methodology workshops, IPC training, and consent-taking sessions. My ability to manage training logistics, prepare materials, and adapt content to the audience's needs has made these programs highly impactful.

Resource Management

Stakeholder Engagement

My work often involves engaging with diverse stakeholders, from senior physicians and department heads to international collaborators. I excel in building relationships, aligning interests, and ensuring transparent communication, which has been critical in driving initiatives forward.

Problem-Solving and Decision-Making

In dynamic and often high-pressure environments, I have demonstrated strong problem-solving skills.
