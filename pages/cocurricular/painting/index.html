Brush and Beyond

Painting and drawing have been my sanctuary for expression, a place where imagination meets reality. With a brush in hand or a pencil on paper, I explore emotions, stories, and the beauty of the world around us. Whether it's a vibrant painting, a delicate sketch, or a spontaneous doodle, each piece reflects a part of my journey and perspective.
In addition to traditional art forms, I have embraced digital creativity, mastering tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Figma. These platforms allow me to blend artistry with technology, creating visually compelling designs that bridge the gap between the tactile and the digital.
On this page, you'll find a mix of my works—spontaneous sketches, intricate paintings, and polished graphic designs. Each creation is a step in my artistic journey, a reflection of my love for storytelling through visuals.

2D Animation

Since childhood, I have been fascinated by 2D and 3D animations. However, I never took it seriously, as it was more of a fun hobby than anything else. Only once, during my third year of medical school, I created a 2D animation on non-communicable diseases for a contest. My friends and I prepared the script for this 2-minute animation, and I took on the role of the animator.
