GRACE Ward

Protocol title:
A quasi-experimental study on the utility of a dedicated Grief Recovery and Care Environment (GRACE) Ward on maternal psychological well-being and adherence to recommended stillbirth management at Faridpur Medical College Hospital, Bangladesh
Role:
Principal Investigator
Duration:
2025-Ongoing
Description:
This study aimed to evaluate the impact of a dedicated GRACE Ward on maternal psychological well-being and adherence to recommended stillbirth management practices. The study was a quasi-experimental design, with the GRACE Ward serving as the intervention group and the control group receiving standard care.
