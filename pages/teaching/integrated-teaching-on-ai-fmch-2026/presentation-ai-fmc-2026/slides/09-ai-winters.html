1974–1993

The AI Winters

When the dream went into hibernation

1974
Lighthill Report

UK review concludes AI has failed. Funding evaporates, labs close globally.

1975–80
First Winter

The early "AI bubble" bursts. Academic research slows to a mere trickle.

1980s
Expert Systems

Brief recovery. MYCIN-style systems attempt commercialization in enterprise.

1987
Expert Systems Collapse

Maintaining complex rules is too expensive. Businesses walk away again.

1993
Winter Ends

Computing power increases. A major shift toward statistical machine learning begins.
