MYCIN — The First AI Doctor

Stanford's expert system for infectious diseases

Led by Edward Shortliffe as part of his PhD thesis in 1972, MYCIN was an expert system — a program built around 600 hand-coded rules about infectious diseases.

This was 1979. The program ran on a mainframe the size of a refrigerator.
