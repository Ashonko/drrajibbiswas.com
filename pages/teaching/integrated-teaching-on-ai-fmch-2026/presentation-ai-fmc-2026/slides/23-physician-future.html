Risks in Your Hands

Questions Before Trusting an AI Responses

"Death by GPS" — A Warning for over-reliance on AI, ignoring real-world cues
1
Does this actually apply to me?
Is the AI using data that fits my specific situation, location, or background?
2
Is it a fact or a guess?
Is there real evidence for this answer, or is the AI just sounding confident?
3
Is the answer consistent?
If I ask the same question in a different way, do I get the same result?
4
Who owns the company behind this AI?
Do they have a history of ethical behavior, or are they known for cutting corners?
